Murray Sell

Artist Bio

I started tattooing in 2002 in Salt Lake City UT. I owned Flying Tiger Tattoo in New Britain CT for 10 years. We recently relocated to beautiful Franklin TN to raise our wonderful daughters around the great friends, family and people here. I specialize in doing what you want when you want it at an affordable price without any BS or rockstar attitudes. Areas of my particular focus of tattooing are Traditional American style tattoos, Japanese traditional style tattoos and imagery that overlaps with the mystical or occult. But whatever you’re into I’m sure I can make it happen and exceed your expectations.
