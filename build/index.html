Free Nipple Areola Tattoos

The finishing touch to breast reconstruction is having your nipple areola tattooed. Tattooing is a simple, fast procedure. There is no need to be scared. If you are matching a new nipple areola to the existing breast, your artist will mix various colors and shades to get the correct pigment. As with any tattoo, the pigment will fade in time. Getting the right shade of color may require more than one tattoo procedure.
